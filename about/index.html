### About the North Country R Users Group (aka NoCoRUG) This group is for folks living in our around New York's "[North Country](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/North_Country_(New_York))" who use the [statistical programming environment R.](https://www.r-project.org/about.html). The group is open to anyone interested in learning and using R and is loosely organized by [Michael Schuckers](https://www.stlawu.edu/people/michael-schuckers) and [Erika Barthelmess](https://www.stlawu.edu/people/erika-barthelmess), professors at [St. Lawrence University](www.stlawu.edu).
